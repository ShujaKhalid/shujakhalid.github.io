Who am I?

Hi! My name is Shuja Khalid and I am currently pursuing a Masters of Science in Applied Computing (MScAC) at the University of Toronto where my focus is Machine Learning and Computer Vision. I received my undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Robotics and Mechatronics from the University of Toronto. Over the years I have worked on a variety of different projects such as mobile/web applications, front-end design, image processing algorithms, computer vision algorithms, machine learning algorithms etc. The purpose of this site is to provide some context for these projects and to capture some of my lessons learned.
